Frequently Asked Questions

How much does flat roof repair cost in Scarsdale, NY?

The cost of flat roof repair in Scarsdale, NY, can vary depending on the extent of the damage, the size of the roof, and the materials used. On average, small repairs can cost between $500 and $2,000, while larger repairs can cost between $2,000 and $10,000 or more.

What are the common causes of flat roof leaks?

Common causes of flat roof leaks include ponding water, cracks and holes in the roof surface, damaged or missing flashing, and worn-out or deteriorated roofing membranes.

Can I repair my flat roof myself?

While it's possible to perform some minor repairs yourself, it's generally recommended to hire a professional roofing contractor to ensure the job is done correctly and safely. Improper repairs can lead to further damage and more costly repairs down the line.
