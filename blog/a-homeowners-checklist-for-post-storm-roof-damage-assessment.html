External Inspection

Before inspecting your roof, make sure to check the exterior of your home for any signs of damage. Look for missing or damaged siding, broken windows, and downed trees or power lines. If you notice any of these issues, it's crucial to address them before inspecting your roof.

Roof Inspection Checklist

  • Missing, damaged, or loose shingles
  • Curling, buckling, or blistering shingles
  • Cracked or broken chimney flashing
  • Damage to roof vents, skylights, or chimneys
  • Signs of wear around chimneys, vents, and skylights

If you notice any of these issues, it's crucial to contact a professional roof repair company like Scarsdale Roof Repair Pros. Our team can provide a thorough inspection and recommend the necessary repairs, including full roof replacement if needed.

Internal Inspection

After inspecting the exterior of your home, it's essential to check the interior for any signs of roof damage. Look for water stains, leaks, or musty smells, which can indicate a compromised roof. If you notice any of these issues, it's crucial to address them promptly to prevent further damage.
